MINING

Our mining solutions range from grass-roots exploration to advanced definition drill-outs. This includes data compilations and reviews, planning, budgeting, drill campaigns with supervision, logistics, project management and implementation. JIL together with its strategic partners offer mining services from exploration to training to mine development.
Our proven local expertise has an international team of experienced personnel representing a range of different culture enabling it to adapt and work anywhere on Earth.
Our consulting services range from the evaluation of mineral properties, quality control implementation, resource estimations, database implementation and economic assessment and review of mineral project.

HIGHLIGHTS OF MINING ACTIVITIES

Desktop Study

We will undertake a literature review and analyse any available data on the region and develop a report on the prospects of the targets.

Mapping and Field Study

We will send a team out to the field to make on site field observations and collect grab samples the identified targeted areas. During this phase we will usually undertake an extensive soil sampling exercise to track any anomalous minerals.

Ground Geophysics

Following the data from the mapping and field study phase we will follow up with geophysics to identity underground structural information. We own all of our own geophysics equipment including, Ground Magnetics, Induced Polarization and Electro Magnetic reading equipment. That data will be used to identify targets.

Drilling

We are proud to own an RC drilling rig that is used for resource definition drilling. Once targets are identified our team can resource drilling. RC drilling is a fast and efficient drilling method that can reduce exploration costs and deliver a quicker completion of drill programs.

RC samples are collected at the drill and sent directly to the assay lab. RC sampling requires much less handling resulting in faster assay turnaround with accompanying cost reductions.
